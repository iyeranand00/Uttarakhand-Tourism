About Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand (literally "Northern Portion" is a North Indian state which is the birthplace of the twin holy rivers Ganga and Yamuna.

Corbett National Park

India's oldest national park, 520 sq km in area, home to the Royal Bengal Tiger. Contains 500 odd plant species and 800+ bird and animal species.
One of the most popular eco-tourism spots in Uttarakhand.

Best Time to Vist:

Summer: Get a good view of all the species and enjoy rafting as well
Winter: Best time to see exotic migratory birds and the Royal Bengal Tiger
